Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026

Your health data is yours. Em~power does not sell it, share it, or use it for advertising. Ever.

Who we are

Em~power is a women's hormone-based fitness and wellness app developed by Emma Scheck, based in Canada. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What we collect

When you use Em~power we collect:

  • Your email address and account credentials
  • Cycle data you enter: last period date, cycle length, phase information
  • Daily logs you submit: energy, mood, symptoms, sleep, heart rate, temperature, flow, pain
  • Cervical mucus observations you log
  • Workout activity you record
  • Body stats you provide: weight, height, fitness level
  • Optional hormone test results you enter manually
  • Feedback you submit through the app

We do not collect location data, contacts, or any data from your device beyond what you type into the app.

How we use your data

Your data is used only to power your personal recommendations inside the app:

  • To calculate your current cycle phase and workout intensity
  • To personalise your nutrition targets based on your body weight and phase
  • To detect patterns in your cycle over time and improve accuracy
  • To show you your own historical data in the calendar and log screens

Your data is never used to train external AI models, never sold to third parties, and never used for advertising.

Where your data is stored

Your data is stored securely in Supabase, a database service hosted on Amazon Web Services infrastructure in Canada and the United States. Supabase uses row-level security, which means your data is technically inaccessible to other users of the app.

Only the developer (Emma Scheck) can access anonymised aggregate patterns for the purpose of improving the app. Individual records are not reviewed unless you report a bug and consent to investigation.

Who can see your data

  • You — only you can see your full data inside the app
  • Emma Scheck (developer) — can access records in the database for bug fixing and app improvement. This access is not routine and is limited to what is necessary.
  • No one else — we do not share your data with health insurers, employers, advertisers, or any third party

Health data

Em~power handles sensitive health information including menstrual cycle data, symptoms, and biometrics. This information is treated with the highest level of care. It is stored encrypted at rest and in transit. It is never shared without your explicit consent.

Em~power is a wellness tracking app, not a medical device. Nothing in this app constitutes medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

To exercise any of these rights, email us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days. Anonymised aggregate statistics (not linked to you) may be retained for app improvement.

Cookies and tracking

Em~power does not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking. The app uses browser local storage only to remember your session and app preferences (such as whether you have dismissed the install banner). No data from local storage is shared externally.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you inside the app before the changes take effect. Continued use of the app after that date means you accept the updated policy.
