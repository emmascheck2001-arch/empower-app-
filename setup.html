How HormoneSync helps you right now
We will put you in observation mode. Daily logging of your discharge, temperature, energy, and mood gives the algorithm the signals it needs to detect when your natural cycle returns.
Watch for discharge changes. Cervical mucus is usually the first sign your cycle is waking up.
Wear your Oura Ring or wearable every night. Temperature shifts are the earliest detectable sign that ovulation is returning.
Track your mood and energy daily. Hormonal shifts show up here before they show up anywhere else.

Your body, your plan
Perimenopause and menopause bring a different hormonal landscape. HormoneSync adapts its recommendations to this phase of life. Workouts, nutrition, and tracking all shift to match where you are.
Early perimenopause, cycles still happening but changing
Late perimenopause, cycles very irregular or mostly gone
Menopause, no period for 12 or more months
Exercise recommendations adapt. Lower estrogen affects muscle recovery, bone density, and how your body uses fuel.
Sleep and mood tracking matter more here. Hot flashes, night sweats, and anxiety are hormonal signals worth logging.
Hormone+ is especially valuable. Salivary cortisol and testosterone testing gives real data on what your body is actually producing.
